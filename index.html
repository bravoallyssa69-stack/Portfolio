Artist Statement

This portfolio represents my journey in understanding visual arts as a powerful form of communication. Throughout the course, I explored different techniques such as collage, drawing, abstract composition, and digital design. Each module allowed me to experiment with elements like color, balance, contrast, and form, helping me express ideas more effectively.

I learned that visuals are not only about aesthetics but also about meaning and message. From creating a digital collage to designing an infographic, I developed both creativity and critical thinking skills. This experience improved my ability to communicate visually and appreciate the role of design in everyday life.
